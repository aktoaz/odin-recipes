Cheesy Eggs

Description

Soft scrambled eggs on toast.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 piece toast of your choice
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps

  1. Put butter in cold pan, heat to medium.
  2. Crack eggs into pan.
  3. When the egg white starts to cook, add cheese to pan.
  4. Scramble throughly.
  5. Toast bread, put on plate and gently place egg mixture on top.
  6. Serve, salt should not be necessary!
