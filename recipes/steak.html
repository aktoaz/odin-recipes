Steak

Description

Steak with seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Beef Tenderloin
  • Montreal Steak seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat grill pan to medium.
  2. Season both sides and place in grill pan.
  3. Sear on all sides, use tongs to hold the steak to sear the edges.
  4. Then allow to cook an equal time on each side.
  5. Remove from pan and let rest on plate for a few minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
