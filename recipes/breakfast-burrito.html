Breakfast Burrito

Description

Burrito with eggs, cheese, potato and sausage.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen burrito

Steps

  1. Open end of burrito plastic wrap.
  2. Throw into microwave.
  3. Press 30 seconds button
  4. Wait a couple minutes.
  5. Remove from microwave with 'flap' of plastic wrap, put on plate and eat!
